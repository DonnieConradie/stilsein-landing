HOW DOES THE STILSEIN NETWORK WORK?

Forget about poor cell reception. StilSein, together with our nationwide network of installers, builds a private long-range radio network right on your farm.

📡

1. The Sensors

We place battery-powered sensors (rain gauges, gates, dams) in the field. The battery lasts up to 10 years.

📻

2. The Gateway

One central base station at the homestead captures radio signals from miles away. No SIM cards required.

Connect Directly

Do you already have The Things Network (TTN) hardware? Fantastic. You don't need any new equipment. Contact us for your unique Farm Key, set up our Webhook in your TTN console, and download the App.

☁️

3. The Cloud

Your data streams securely through our servers to your isolated database where Jakkals-AI analyzes it.

📱

4. The App

Everything displays instantly and neatly organized on your phone. You get warnings even before a crisis hits.

THE BRAINS BEHIND THE OPERATION

MEET JAKKALS AI.

While our headquarters guards the hardware, Jakkals analyzes your data.

Jakkals is not a generic "chatbot". At installation, he is calibrated for your specific farm. He knows your exact farming type, specific rainfall region, distances between your camps, the nearest town, and your farm's unique flood thresholds.

  • ✓ Proactive Warnings: Analyzes Open-Meteo satellite data against your farm's thresholds to predict heatwaves or storms days in advance.
  • ✓ Context-Aware: He knows whether 15mm of rain is a relief for your sheep, or if it poses a flood risk for crops.
  • ✓ Your Personal Assistant: Ask him questions in the app, and instantly get a farming-specific answer based on today's numbers.
